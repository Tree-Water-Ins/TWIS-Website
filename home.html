Solutions For Your Business

We at Tree Water Insurance Solutions, design and implement cost-containing group health insurance strategies for employer groups without compromising access or quality of care. Our strategic partnership with health insurance carriers, stop-loss providers, and third-party administrators affords us the ability to install and manage major medical and self-funded benefit offerings, employer-paid ancillary coverages, and work-site voluntary plans.

We help C-suite professionals design or restructure employee benefits plans, evaluate existing broker relationships, analyze rate history vs claims experience, and more. Deficiencies in any one or more of these critical areas could lead to overspending, which may affect the company's financial solvency.
